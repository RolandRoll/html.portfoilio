About Me

My name is Chun-Shing Au, a theatremaker from Hong Kong. My work explores the intersection of visual poetics and political context through object theatre, mask, and puppetry performance. My artistic journey has been shaped by my experiences in Hong Kong, Canada, and Prague before settling in the Netherlands in 2022 to attend DAS Theatre (formerly DasArts), where I graduated in 2024.

Together with my wife, Carmen Lee, I co-founded Theatre du Poulet. For nearly a decade, we have pushed the boundaries of objects and devised theatre. Beyond ‘theatre’ or ‘puppetry’, our practice also extends to public spaces and engaging non-professional performers such as community members. Additionally, we frequently reflect on the dynamics of working as artistic collaborators while being life partners.
